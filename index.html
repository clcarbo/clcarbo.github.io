ABOUT

The internet has irrevocably transformed how people make decisions. Today, we have more information at our disposal than any other time in human history. Yet without the proper tools and expertise to interpret this information, it is impossible for us to make informed choices.

My time at the University of Notre Dame has prepared me for providing this expertise. During my time as a student, I have received training in:

  • Using cutting edge modeling techniques and machine learning algorithms
  • Working with databases, web frameworks, and more
  • Communicating findings to both technical and non-technical audiences

I have used these skills while working at a startup, as a research assistant, and as a student. My areas of expertise include natural language processing tasks, social network analysis, applied predictive modeling, and research document production. I also have experience with web development and commandline application development.

When I am not working, you can usually find me reading post-modern fiction, recommending music to people, and playing Super Smash Bros. Melee.
